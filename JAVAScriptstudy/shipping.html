Shipping Information

Welcome to Noir Vault's shipping information page. We aim to provide you with the best possible service, ensuring your orders are processed and delivered in a timely manner.

Shipping Methods and Costs

We offer various shipping options to suit your needs. Shipping costs are calculated based on the delivery address and the weight of your order.

  • Standard Shipping: Delivered within 5-7 business days. Free for orders over $100.
  • Express Shipping: Delivered within 2-3 business days. Additional charges apply.
  • Overnight Shipping: Delivered the next business day. Additional charges apply.

Order Processing Time

Orders are processed and shipped within 1-2 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once your order has been dispatched, along with tracking information.

International Shipping

We ship to select international destinations. Shipping times and costs vary based on location. Please note that customers are responsible for any customs duties or taxes imposed by their country.

Tracking Your Order

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track the progress of your shipment through our courier's website.

Shipping Restrictions

We are unable to ship to PO Boxes, APO/FPO addresses, and certain remote locations. If you have any questions regarding shipping restrictions, please contact our customer service team.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our shipping policies, please do not hesitate to contact our customer service team at support@noirvault.com.
